Welcome to KeySwitch

This installer will install KeySwitch, a background daemon that automatically configures your Keychron keyboard layout when it connects to your Mac.

What will be installed:

  • KeySwitch daemon program
  • Automatic startup configuration (LaunchAgent)

What it does:

  • Runs in the background automatically
  • Detects when your Keychron keyboard connects (e.g., after KVM switch)
  • Sets the keyboard to Mac mode automatically
